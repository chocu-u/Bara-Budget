Bara-Budget: The Savage Financial Coach
Stop being an accountant and start being a human. Bara-Budget is the only financial tracker that knows you are broke, knows you are impulsive, and judges you for it.

The Vibe
Budgeting apps are boring. They are full of clinical spreadsheets and judgmental colors. You do not need a calculator to tell you that you are poor. You need a best friend to tell you that you are making a terrible life choice at 2 AM.

How It Works
The Setup: You input your weekly budget (e.g., 500 PHP).

The Impulse: You tell the app you want to buy milktea, grab, or another Gunpla kit.

The Roast: Our AI agent analyzes your financial status and hits you with a response.

The Visuals: We show you the "Brokenness-O-Meter" and the "Sacrifice Chart" to show exactly what you lose if you make this purchase.

Key Features
1. Roast Modes
Choose how you want to be treated:

The Savage Roast: Brutally honest. It will tell you that your spending habits are the reason you are still single.

The Enabler (Guy Math): It tries to justify your bad decisions. "It is only 150 pesos. If you do not buy it, you are basically saving money for the apocalypse."

The Anxious Parent: "Are you sure? What if you run out of load? Think of your future, anak."

2. The Brokenness-O-Meter
We ditched the pie charts. We use a custom gauge that ranges from "Sana All" to "GCash Zero" to "Mangungutang na sa tropa."

3. The Sacrifice Chart
Ever wonder what that milktea costs you in real terms? The app generates a funny infographic showing exactly how many commute rides or meals you are sacrificing for that one cup of sugar.
